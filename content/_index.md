# The Worst Haiku You've Ever Heard

### A Rom-Com Novella

---

## The Story

**Nora Castillo** (29, veterinary technician) and **Eli Barker** (31, recently laid-off copywriter) are forced into each other's orbits when their elderly neighbor, **June Matsuda**, breaks her hip and begs them both to co-foster her enormous, anxious Great Dane, **Captain**.

The problem: Captain has separation anxiety so severe he can't be alone for more than twenty minutes without destroying furniture. They need each other. They do not want to need each other.

## About the Book

Alternating dual first-person POV with Eli's chapters ending in (genuinely bad) haikus. ~24 chapters exploring how two people learn that being loved isn't a reward for being useful—and that it's okay to just be.

*Featuring:* friendship, neurotic dogs, terrible poetry, Sunday dinners, and one very squeaky taco.
